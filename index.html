The Black Business Research Table

A public database of Black-owned businesses in the United States, built from public records, city and state MWBE listings, and open data sources. It is designed for researchers, journalists, policymakers, and anyone who wants to understand the landscape of Black-owned enterprise in America.

The database is free to access. To download the full dataset, send a request using the form below with your name, affiliation, and intended use. Please cite the database as: McCullers, K. (2026). Black Business Research Table [Database]. Retrieved from blackbusinessresearchtable.com.
